BATTLE BY FERMI

Designed by Enrico Fermi and Remus Lupin

The ultimate wand.

Using nuclear technology to break down the three superior cores; unicorn hair, phoenix feather, and dragon heartstring; down to their magical properties, the Ultimate Wand combines all of these to create the most magically potent wand in existence, more so than the Elder Wand.


On top of this, the wand also has a core of uranium, making it possible for the wielder to utlise the nuclear properties of this element as a weapon. The properties are harnessed through magic, and of course, the wielder is also protected from any radioactivy.

An unbreakable gauntlet.

Made through dwarvish construction and precision, our gauntlet is encrusted with five stones: uranium, polonium, tritium, actinium, and finally a common rock. The rock represents and channels the power of the Resurrection Stone via the energy from the radioactive elements.


The gauntlet is constructed out of lead to protect the wearer. When utilized correctly, the gauntlet can render even the greatest of wands useless via it's nuclear energy.

A nuclear armour.

The armour is composed of a thin, fibrous layer with uranium spread out over it. The nuclear energy absorbs all magical energy directed towards it, protecting the wearer from magical attacks.


Its uranium lining not only absorbs, but also stores and expels and energy it takes in. This is achieved by making a rare alloy of uranium and steel achieved by Lupin's mastery of alchemy.

The Fermi broomstick.

The Fermi broomstick features a uranium powered 'pocket' reactor at its end, allowing it to fly at previously unimaginable speeds. In addition, the Thestral hairs winding through the broom's core provide it with complete invisbility free from the effects of any disillusionment charms.
